# Video Colorist Notes

Director/style research: search interviews, cinematographer notes, official stills/trailers, ASC/BSC/film society pieces, and reputable visual analysis. Extract only practical traits: rhythm, palette, contrast, lighting, texture, sound, crop/lens feel.

Multi-clip editing: inspect all clips, group by subject/motion/quality, write outline, make cut list, render, verify final contact sheet.

Preset intent:

- `balanced-clean`: natural cleanup.
- `soft-clear`: cool clarity for haze/low contrast.
- `cold-film`: deeper cool shadows and blue/cyan mood.
- `japanese-cold-film`: low saturation, lifted blacks, quiet air.
- `warm-film`: warm highlights, nostalgic contrast.
